My Work

I have worked on a multitude of projects including a mobile application that teachs kids how cook as well as application that allows students to travel from city to city in a more effective manner

CINNAMONSTER

Upon completing some market research it is evident that there a gap in terms educational apps that teach children to cook. To solve this issue Me and a team of designers set out to create an kid-freindly application that caters towards kids. This application allows children from the ages of 11-14 to interact with a plethora of recipes and get accustomed to cooking at an early age. It gives children with allegeries more options when it comes to school lunches. It also solves the nutrition crisis that plagues the country and allows children to create and consume healtier foods.

CITYCONNECT

The idea for this app came when a friend of mine told me about the hardships of trying to find a carpool to travel back home as he is a student that lives on campus. To solve this issue me and group of peers decided to think of a way to tackle this issue. Currently the only solid way to travel from city to city is by joing a plethora of facebook groups in which drivers will post a trip. However this method proves to be quite tedious and drivers are not always reliable. By using a similar platform as uber and lyft we devised a way to inlcude these options by allowing drivers to post ads on our app and automatically finding them riders using a machine learning algorithim
